What is YPSA?

The Youth Parliament of the Swiss Abroad (YPSA) is an organization dedicated to connecting and advocating for young Swiss citizens living outside of Switzerland. As a vibrant and inclusive platform, YPSA ensures that the voices, experiences, and challenges of young Swiss expatriates are heard, valued, and addressed.

YPSA is the youth branch of the Organisation of the Swiss Abroad (OSA).

Our Purpose

At YPSA, our mission is to empower and support young Swiss abroad through various initiatives and opportunities. Here's how we do it:

  • Building a Global Community

    We bring together young Swiss expatriates from all over the world, fostering a supportive and dynamic network. By joining YPSA, you become part of a global community where you can reconnect with your Swiss heritage, share your journey, and build lasting friendships.

  • Providing a Platform for Exchange

    Our platform is designed to encourage open dialogue, allowing members to share their personal stories, insights, and experiences. By promoting collaboration, we create opportunities for cultural and professional growth.

  • Raising Awareness

    We are committed to highlighting the unique needs, opportunities, and challenges faced by young Swiss abroad. Through targeted campaigns, collaborations with the press, and outreach efforts, we ensure that the voice of the Swiss youth is heard both in Switzerland and internationally.

  • Promoting Engagement

    YPSA organizes a variety of activities and events, ranging from educational workshops to cultural exchanges. Whether you're looking to learn, network, or simply have fun, our events are tailored to inspire and connect.
